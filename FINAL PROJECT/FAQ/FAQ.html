Do I have to be a professional football player to join the team?

No, anyone who is keen on football and respects Ukraine can join us.

Are there any age restrictions?

The minimum age for participation in the tournament is 18 years. There is no maximum age restriction.

Do you have an online shop?

By writing to this e-mail address, you can order football kits for adults and children - fc_ukrajina_meunchen@gmx.de

What are your top 3 goals for this season?

To continue volunteering, get promoted from the third league to the second, win the league cup.

Where and when do the trainings take place?

Every Friday at 7pm. Fußballplatz am Westbad - Silberdistelstraße, 81241 München
